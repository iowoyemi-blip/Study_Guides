# Unit 6 Review Dashboard - Teacher Review Notes

The dashboard was built from the Unit 6 study guide packet and teacher key.

## Source Files

- Student packet: `Unit 6 Test Study Guide - New Format DRAFT.pdf`
- Teacher key: `Unit 6 Test Study Guide - New Format DRAFT KEY.docx`

The source PDF, DOCX, and answer key files were not changed.

## Included Problems

The app includes all 28 worked study guide entries extracted from the teacher key.

The packet page preview uses rendered images from the 8-page student study guide.

## Similar Practice

The similar-practice section now generates outside-the-packet practice instead of routing students to the matching Version A / Version B companion problem.

Each major Unit 6 skill has at least four similar prompts available through the `New Similar Problem` button:

- identifying polynomial and non-polynomial expressions
- classifying polynomials and writing standard form
- finding and factoring out greatest common factors
- multiplying binomials and larger polynomial expressions
- adding and subtracting polynomials
- factoring trinomials, differences of squares, and perfect-square trinomials
- solving quadratic equations by factoring

## Answer-Key Notes

No unresolved answer-key issues were found during the automated build. Answers and worked steps came from the teacher key.

The typed checker for similar-practice items is intentionally lightweight. It accepts common compact formats, but equivalent expression, graph, coordinate, interval, or inequality notation may still need students to reveal the answer and compare manually.
