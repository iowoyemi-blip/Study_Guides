`Teaching-Studio/90-Archive/2025-2026/Instructional-Materials/Algebra-I_On_Level-Materials/Unit 2 - Expressions, Equations, and Inequalities/Assessments/`

The source PDF, DOCX, and answer key files were not changed.

## Included Problems

The app includes all 52 study guide entries:

- Multiple-choice practice: 1A-9B
- Free-response practice: 10-A through 17d-B

The packet page preview uses rendered images from the 15-page student study guide. Pages 1-14 contain the practice problems, and page 15 contains the self-check answer bank.

## Answer-Key Notes

No unresolved answer-key issues were found during the build. Answers and worked steps came from the teacher key.

The typed checker for similar-practice items is intentionally lightweight. It accepts common compact formats, but equivalent expression, interval, or inequality notation may still need students to reveal the answer and compare manually.

## Student Workflow

1. Students complete problems on their paper packet first.
2. Students click the problem number in the dashboard.
3. Students reveal the final answer first.
4. Students reveal the steps only after checking their own work.
5. Students can open similar practice when they need another aligned problem.
