Algebra 1 Unit 2 Comprehensive Practice Quiz

Section 1: Order of Operations

1. Solve: 3 + 4 × 2 - 1

2. Solve: 15 - 2(3² - 5) + 4

3. Solve: |7 - 9| + 2 × 3² - |4 - 8|

4. Solve: (24 ÷ 6 + 2 × 3) ÷ (5 - 2² + 3)

Section 2: Translating Phrases into Algebraic Expressions

1. Write an algebraic expression for: Three times a number

2. Write an algebraic expression for: The sum of twice a number and seven

3. Write an algebraic expression for: Five less than the product of a number and four

4. Write an algebraic expression for: The quotient of a number decreased by three and eight

Section 3: Evaluating Expressions

1. Evaluate 2x + 5 for x = 3

2. Evaluate x² - 3x + 1 for x = -1

3. Evaluate 3a - 2b + 4 for a = -1 and b = 3

4. Evaluate (x² + y) ÷ (2x - 1) for x = 2 and y = -3

Section 4: Formulating Algebraic Equations from Word Problems

1. A number increased by 12 equals 35. Find the number.

2. The sum of three consecutive integers is 48. Find the first integer.

3. A rectangle's width is 4 cm less than its length. If the perimeter is 32 cm, find the width.

4. A cell phone plan costs $25 per month plus $0.10 per text message. If the monthly bill was $31.50, how many text messages were sent?

2. Solve: 2(x - 4) + 5 = 13

3. Solve: 4x - 3(x + 2) = 8

4. Solve: (2x + 6) ÷ 3 = x - 4

2. Determine the solution type: 3(x - 2) = 3x - 6

3. Determine the solution type: 4x + 7 = 2x + 15

4. Determine the solution type: 5(x + 1) - 2x = 3x + 5

2. Solve A = (1/2)bh for h

3. Solve 3x + 2y = 12 for y

Section 8: Solving Multi-step Inequalities

1. Solve: 2x + 3 > 11

2. Solve: -3x + 5 ≤ 14

3. Solve: 4(x - 2) < 3x + 1

4. Solve: (2x - 6) ÷ 3 ≥ x + 2

2. Solve: 3x - 1 ≤ 8 or x + 5 > 12

3. Solve: -2 < x + 1 < 5

4. Solve: 2x + 3 ≥ 7 and 4x - 1 < 15
